1. The Atom

Maharishi Kanad & The Parmanu Theory

In 500BC, Maharishi Kanad a prominent Indian Philospher was puzzled by the question what is matter made up of?, he started of by cutting a peice of Gold into half and he kept on going, eventually he reached a point where he could not cut the Gold Block further on, and called the small gold block particles as Parmanu, which means Atom.

Around the same era, two philosphers Democritus & Leucippus postulated the same, and Democritus called these particles as Atomos - Indivisible.
Thus, Atomos or Atoms make up matter which we see around us.
