1. The Atom

Zooming into the Atom

Did you imagine something like this?
At the centre of an Atom we have a blob called as the Nucleus which is made up of two tiny particles called as Protons(represented in blue) & Neutrons(represented in red), also buzzing around them in orbits at an incredibly high speed are Electrons(represented in green)

But why do the Electrons buzz around the nucleus, can't they just fly away?
Actually Electrons are negatively charged and Protons are positively charged hence they attract each other and Neutrons have no charge, so they are neutral.

So why does the electron not slam into the nucleus?
The electrons move at such a high speed, that they miss to hit the nucleus, actually the satellites which orbit our Earth have the same exact phenomenon, that they miss to hit Earth, hence they stay in their orbit.


Now both Proton & Neutron are very similar, both have same mass and size,except their charges, but the electron is very different from the two in matters of shape and size an electron is so small and light weight than the protons and neutrons that even considering them while weighing an atom is meaningless, the same way in which you dont care about the weight of a ring, or your clothes while measureing your own weight on a scale.

Now we have something important, if the atom was enlarged so that the nucleus becomes the size of a grape then the electrons will be a mile awayso most of the space inside an atom is just empty
